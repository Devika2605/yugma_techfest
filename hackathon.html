Hackathon Guidelines

Team Composition

Teams should consist of 2-4 members. All team members must be currently enrolled in a college/university.

Development

All code must be written during the hackathon. You can use any programming language, framework, or tools.

Equipment

Participants must bring their own laptops and any hardware they plan to use. Power and Wi-Fi will be provided.

Schedule

The hackathon will run for 24 hours continuously. Teams must be present for the entire duration.

Judging Criteria

Projects will be judged on innovation, technical complexity, usability, and potential impact.

Food & Breaks

Meals and snacks will be provided. There will be designated break areas.
