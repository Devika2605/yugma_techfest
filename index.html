About

About the College

JNNCE, established in 1980 by NES in Shivamogga, is affiliated to VTU and accredited by NAAC (A Grade) and NBA. With a 55-acre campus, modern facilities, and strong placement records, it fosters excellence in engineering, research, and entrepreneurship.

About the Department

Started in 1999, the ISE department offers top-notch infrastructure and encourages MOOC learning. It manages campus networking and nurtures highly skilled IT professionals.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in Information Science and Engineering, producing innovative and globally competent professionals.

Mission

Delivering quality education, fostering research, and inspiring students to excel in IT through collaboration and higher learning.
